Gain Insights from your Documents using NLP

In today's fast-paced world of Information Technology and Digital Transformation, we are spending a lot of time to create, organize, find and digest documents. They can be extensive reports, manuals, meeting notes, technical documents, etc., and managing them can be time-consuming and challeging. Fortunately, advancements in Natural Language Processing (NLP) have opened doors to a new era of efficiency and insight. In this article, we delve into the innovative methodology behind a document-analysis application that leverages the capabilities of NLP to transform your daily operations.

Methodology

Without further adue, let's dive in the solution that is presented in the following Figure.

Step 1: Data Collection
Begin with your raw documents that capture the relevant content of your organization.
Step 2: Data Filtering and Cleaning
Streamline the data by removing unnecessary columns and document parts when not needed, identifying different formats like tables, different languages, and preprocessing data, ensuring a clean foundation for analysis.
Step 3: Sentence Transformer Embeddings
Enter the realm of deep learning, where solution text is transformed into a high-dimensional space. Here, NLP truly flexes its muscles, converting text into numerical values represented by vectors that can be used in mathematical calculations.
Step 4: UMAP Dimension Reduction
Transition from the high-dimensional complexity to a concise five-dimensional space with UMAP's dimension reduction technique, simplifying the data while preserving its essence.
Step 5: HDBSCAN Clustering
Embrace the versatility of HDBSCAN to cluster the contents into similar topics based on the reduced dimensions, offering insights without imposing specific structural assumptions on clusters.
Step 6: Topic Labeling
Emerge from the data analysis with clear, representative names and words for each cluster, providing a meaningful context to the insights.
Step 7: Cluster Enrichment and Elasticsearch
Enhance the data's depth by incorporating named entities, and store this enriched dataset in Elasticsearch, creating a robust database.

High-Level Magic of NLP Transformation

At the heart of our NLP-application lies the concept of Sentence Transformer Embeddings. These remarkable tools have their roots in the field of deep learning, and convert text into multi-dimensional data vectors. Originally inspired by BERT (Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers), these embeddings offer a significant new approach to textual data analysis. By representing text as numerical vectors, we unlock the ability to quantify and compare the subtle nuances hidden within our documents. This transformation provides a foundation for similarity search based on queries as well as clustering, enabling us to retrieve knowledge (information), group similar text together and reveal patterns that would otherwise be difficult to extract manually.

Illustrating Insights with Kibana

Finally, we enter the world of business intelligence for advanced data visualization and exploration tools, like Kibana. This tool allows us to monitor and report the main findings of the NLP analysis using dynamic bars, charts, intuitive dashboards and other engaging formats. This accessibility to data visualization enhances the clarity of our findings and their practical utility. Using Kibana dashboards our organization can spot trends, identify bottlenecks, and make informed decisions in real time, ensuring that the value derived from the document analysis translates into actionable insights to improve efficiency and productivity.

Conclusion

In conclusion, we've unraveled the remarkable journey of harnessing NLP's power for IT ticket analysis. By following this methodology, organizations can unlock valuable insights, streamline support operations, and enhance customer satisfaction. The future of IT support has arrived, and it's driven by the magic of NLP.
